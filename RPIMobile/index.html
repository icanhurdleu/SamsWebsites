RPI MOBILE

An app that will help you with your day to day life as a student at RPI. Read below to learn more.

ABOUT

RPI mobile is an app made by members of Rensselaer's Center for Open-Source Software. The app is an effort to streamline a lot of the information provided by RPI. RPI Mobile is available for both iPhone and Android, but each version is unique.

Scroll through the website to explore the different parts of RPI Mobile in detail. Hover over the menu icon on the left to expand the quick navigation links to jump around the site. Find contact information at the bottom to send comments and suggestions to the app or website developers.

ATHLETICS

The athletics section contains all the men's and women's sports, and you can toggle between the two using the gender selector in the top right. After clicking on a team, you can view more specific details such as:

  • Roster
  • Schedule
  • News
  • Records

Hover over a list item to get more information.

SOCIAL FEED

Want to make sure you stay up to date with what's happening at RPI? Make sure you check this section often as it updates with the latest tweets from RPI associated accounts.

Check out the tweets from Rensselaer's account, RPI Athletics, and RPI news.

MAP

Ever had trouble finding a building on campus? You can look up any RPI building and each building is categorized and marked with a specific color to make navigating easier.

Additionally, if you enable GPS on your phone, the app will provide walking directions to a building of your choice, so you can arrivce as soon as possible.

LAUNDRY

Tired of arriving at the laundry room only to discover that all the washers or dryers are in use? The laundry status lets you quickly and easily see how many machines are available/in use at a certain location.

MORNING MAIL

Need to check Morning Mail on the go? The app displays the mail headline and associated leading text to allow you to easily browse through the topics of the day. You can then click on the section to view more details

The app also makes it simple to check mail from previous dates. Simply drag the screen to change the day and find what you are looking for.

DIRECTORY

The directory is inconvenient to access on the web, and a lot of times you need to find information on the go. All you have to do is type in a full name or part of a name and the app will filter the results. If you click on a name, you can then see the person's major, rpi email address, and class year.
